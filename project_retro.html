Overview

Alchemy Academy is a turn-based monster-fusion roguelike RPG in which the player begins as a newly enrolled alchemist student and gradually grows into a master alchemist. The core loop centers on exploring elemental wilderness maps, defeating monsters, gathering alchemical materials, fusing custom pets, and using those pets to progress through academy trials, professor challenges, and main story encounters.

Gameplay Loop

The core loop is designed around repeated growth and experimentation:

Explore → Gather Materials → Fuse Pets → Strengthen Team → Clear Story Challenges → Unlock New Areas → Explore Again

Main Features
  • Monster Fusion System: Create pets by combining body, head, legs, arms, back, and tail from different monsters.
  • Body-Part-Driven Skills: Each part grants stats, abilities, and influences future skill learning.
  • Shared Mana System: The whole team draws from one mana pool, making every action a strategic choice.
  • Academy Progression: Challenge classmates and professors to unlock stronger content and new regions.
Combat System

Combat is a turn-based battle system with positional movement, speed-based turn order, physical and magical damage, and elemental strengths and weaknesses.

  • All pets act once per round
  • Turn order is determined by Speed
  • The whole team gains 50 Mana each turn
  • All skill costs are paid from one shared mana pool
  • Players must balance offense, defense, setup, and support
Fusion System

Fusion is the heart of the game. Every pet is built from multiple body parts: Body, Head, Legs, Arms, Back, and Tail.

The Body determines the pet’s main stat identity, while other parts provide bonus stats, elemental alignment, granted skills, and special effects. Using parts of the same element improves the chance of producing a higher-talent pet, while mixed-element fusion increases variety and strategic creativity.

    Skill Rarity & Level-Up Growth

    Skills in Alchemy Academy are divided into rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Legendary.

    Skill rarity affects how often a skill appears during level-up. Common skills appear frequently, while Rare and Legendary skills are much harder to obtain.

    Body parts increase the chance of their granted skills appearing in the pet’s level-up skill pool. This means fusion choices influence both immediate combat identity and long-term growth.

    Rarity Appearance Rate Typical Role
    Common High Basic attacks, buffs, simple utility
    Uncommon Medium Special movement, hybrid utility, setup tools
    Rare Low Strong AoE, control effects, impactful mechanics
    Legendary Very Low Build-defining or game-changing skills
    Mana System

    Mana is shared by the whole team rather than assigned to individual monsters. This is one of the game’s core strategic systems.

    Because mana is shared, players must decide which pet should spend resources each round. This creates strong tension between offense, defense, support, and setup skills.

    World Structure

    The game is divided into two major spaces:

    • The Academy: hub for healing, shopping, professor battles, and progression
    • The Wilderness: elemental maps such as Lake, Grassland, Volcano, Mine, and Desert

    Each wilderness region contains different elemental monster populations and supports different fusion strategies.
