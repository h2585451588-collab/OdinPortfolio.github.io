Type: Horror

Concept

Deadcast is a first-person co-op horror multiplayer game. In this game you will become a contestants in a horror-themed game show, and your goal is not just to survive, but to put on a show and rack up points while doing so!

What's the Game like? What we have donwn so far?
Gameplay Loop

The core loop is designed around exploration, selling and getting more:

Explore → Gather items → selling the items to get money → buying items → killing monsters → Unlock New Areas → Explore Again
